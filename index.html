Welcome to SLS Oil Industries Solvent and Refinery!

We welcome you to Sri Lakshmi Srinivasa Oil Industries, SLS Oil Industries is a partnership firm owned and operated by Chitturi Srinivas and four collegues in the city of Sindhanur, Karnataka. SLS Oil Industries enter the rice bran oil manufacturing business. Rice bran (RB) is a nutrient-rich by-product of the rice milling process. It consists of pericarp, seed coat, nucellus, and aleurone layer. RB is a rich source of a protein, fat, dietary fibers, vitamins, minerals, and phytochemicals (mainly oryzanols and tocopherols), and is currently mostly used as animal feed. SLS Oil Industries focusing to farmers growth and rural develoment.
